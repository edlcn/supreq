Prerequisite Chain of Sabanci University Courses

Quick Guide

- You can search for course(s), just seperate them with a comma. (ex. query: CS432,EE411)

- You can search for course domain(s). (ex. query: ENS, EE, CS)

- You can combine both.

- The data might be corrupted for some reason, double check what you see here by double clicking the course node.

- You can zoom-in or zoom-out to have a better sight.

- There might be logical issues with the graph, due to bad prerequisite entries on bannerweb.

- There might be other restrictions, this application only tries to visualize the chain.
